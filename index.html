Our Story/

We are Lee and Chloe, we converted an old Station Master’s house into three luxury self-contained holiday homes. Along the journey, came its problems but the sacrifices paid off when we see our guests’ enjoyment at our properties. Something that becomes so sentimental also brings along worries.

To open the doors of your property for strangers to treat as a home from home, is both daunting and exhilarating at the same time. Whether your accommodation is a pod, a castle, or anything in between, we want to reduce repetitive questions, save time and provide additional security to property owners so that you can relax and enjoy it in the same way that your guests do.
